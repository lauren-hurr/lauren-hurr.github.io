Hello there! It’s nice to meet you!

I’m Lauren, a UX/UI designer who strives to create delightful experiences that leave a positive impact on people and businesses.

I believe that a great designer is someone that is not only creative, but also knows how to design and problem solve for both business and user needs.

I attended Parsons School of Design with a major in Fine Arts. Though I loved art, I wanted to pursue design-led research and strategy to put my analytical side to use, so I switched my major to Strategic Design and Management. Through this major, I learned about design-led research methods, brand strategy, information visualization, and product development.

After graduating, I worked at Universal McCann (UM) as an Associate Product Media Strategist on the Shopper team, where we worked with leading clients such as Hershey, Johnson & Johnson, and Coca-Cola. My experience working at this role has sharpened my analytical, communication, and data presentation skills.

Throughout my education and career journey, I learned that my true passions were with learning about people and creating functional products that make a positive impact on people’s lives. I realized that I didn’t need to choose between my creative and analytical side, since UX/UI design touches on both of those skillsets. This leads to me to where I am now, pursuing a career that I truly love.
