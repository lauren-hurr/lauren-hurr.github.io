Hello there! It’s nice to meet you!

I’m Lauren, a UX/UI Designer who strives to create innovative solutions for complex problems by taking time to learn and empathize with users.

My background in both fine arts and design-led research has helped me with my transition into UX/UI design. I attended Parsons School of Design where I first majored in Fine Arts and transitioned into Strategic Design. I love the arts, but my true passions were with learning about users and creating products that help make people’s lives easier. This led me to pursue UX/UI design where I can combine my artistic side with creating solutions that’s backed up by data.

When I’m not designing, you can catch me playing with my dog or avoiding avocados.
